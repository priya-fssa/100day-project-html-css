MUTTON CHUKKA

INGREDIENTS:











STEPS:
  1. Lets prepare masala for mutton, powder Cumin Seeds, Pepper corns, Dry red chili, coriander seeds in a blender and grind it to a fine powder and keep it aside
  2. In a pressure cooker add the mutton pieces, along with the lamb add turmeric powder, freshly ground mutton masala(in step 1), required salt with 1/2 cup of water and cook for 3 whistles.
  3. In a pan heat some oil and add green chilies , curry leaves, shallots, salt and saute until shallots are golden and then add 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste and saute it for a couple of minutes until the raw smell is gone
  4. Add the cooked mutton pieces and cook for some more time until all the water has been evaporated and the mutton become a little dry
  5. Delicious Mutton Roast / Mutton chukka is ready.. enjoy this side with any rice varieties and also goes excellent with Dosa , puri and Chappathi ...Happy Cooking!!!
