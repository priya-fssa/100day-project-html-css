Chickenfry recipe

INGREDIENTS:









STEPS:
  1. Clean wash and cut the chicken as cubes
  2. Add all the other ingredients give above to chicken and mix well. Add few drops of water if required
  3. Mix well so it is nicely combined
  4. Heat oil in kadai and cook the chicken till crispy and golden in color.
  5. Serve hot chicken fry.
  6. Squeeze some lime juice and enjoy the tangy chicken fry.
