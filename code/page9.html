MASALA PURI

INGREDIENTS:





















STEPS:
  1. firstly, in a large bowl take 1 cup white peas and soak in enough water for 8 hours.
  2. take soaked peas in the cooker and add 1 potato, ½ tsp turmeric and ½ tsp clove.
  3. add 3 cup water and pressure cook for 6 whistles.
  4. mash the potato slightly, adjusting the consistency as required. keep aside.
  5. now prepare masala paste by heating, 2 tbsp oil and saute 1 tsp fennel.
  6. also add 1 onion, 1 tsp ginger garlic paste and saute slightly.
  7. further, add 1 tomato and saute until the tomatoes turn soft and mushy.
  8. cool completely and transfer to a blender.
  9. add ½ cup mint and ¾ cup coriander.
  10. blend to smooth paste adding water if required. keep aside.
  11. In a large kadai heat 2 tbsp oil and ¾ tsp cumin, 1 bay leaf and 1 inch cinnamon. saute until spices turn aromatic.
  12. now add ½ tsp chilli powder, ½ tsp coriander powder, ¼ tsp cumin powder and ½ tsp garam masala.
  13. saute on low flame until spices turn aromatic.
  14. further, add in boiled white peas and mix well.
  15. add 1 cup water and 1 tsp salt.
  16. mix well adjusting consistency as required.
