Fish fry

INGREDIENTS:










STEPS:
  1. Into a bowl mix turmeric powder,coriander powder,chilli powder,ginger garlic paste,curry leaves,salt,pepper powder and lemon juice
  2. coat the fish pieces well with the masalas and marinate it for minimum 20-30 minutes
  3. Heat some oil and fry until crispy on both the sides
  4. Delicious fish fry is ready enjoy it with rice :) Happy Cooking
